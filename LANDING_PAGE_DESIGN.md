
# Artisan AI - "Galaxy of Tools" Design Spec
> Generated by Stitch | Vibe: Industrial Powerhouse, Max-Feature Showcase.

## 1. Visual Strategy: "The Creative Arsenal"
- **Goal**: Show that Artisan AI is not just a tool, but an entire *factory* for creative output.
- **Hook**: "Why pay $200/mo for 5 different apps when you can own the entire production line?"

## 2. Structure Overview
1.  **Hero**: "The Ultimate AI Production Line". 3D visuals of books, t-shirts, and logos floating in space.
2.  **The "Problem" (Cost) Calculator**: 
    - Interactive comparison: Jasper ($49) + Midjourney ($30) + Vellum ($30) = **$109/mo**.
    - Artisan: **$0 upfront. Pay per output.**
3.  **The "Arsenal" (Feature Grid)**:
    - **Publishing**: KDP Book Lab (Writing + Layouts + Covers).
    - **Merch**: POD Designer (T-Shirt Graphics).
    - **Marketing**: Marketing Copywriter (Emails, Ads).
    - **Design**: Logo Creator, Object Isolator, HD Upscaler.
    - **Intelligence**: Niche Radar, Trend Intelligence, KDP Ban Shield.
4.  **Visual Proof**:
    - "Real outputs, not mockups." Show actual 3D rendered books and apparel.
5.  **Final CTA**: "Launch The Studio".

## 3. Detailed Components
- `CostComparator.tsx`: A slider showing money saved per year.
- `ToolShowcase.tsx`: A tabbed interface switching between "Writer", "Designer", "Marketer", "Strategist".
- `GalaxyGrid.tsx`: A dense, bento-box style grid of every single tool icon + one-line description.
