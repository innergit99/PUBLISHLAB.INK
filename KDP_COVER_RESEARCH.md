## üìä GENRE-SPECIFIC COVER FORMULAS (WHAT ACTUALLY SELLS)

### **1. MYSTERY/THRILLER**
‚úÖ **Elements that convert**:
- **Dark, moody palettes** (deep blues, blacks, blood reds)
- **High contrast** (80%+ contrast ratio)
- **Sans-serif titles** (Impact, Bebas Neue, Oswald)
- **Gritty textures** (rain, fog, shadows)
- **Focal point**: Silhouette, weapon, or ominous object
- **Typography**: BOLD, all caps, kerning tight

‚ùå **Common failures**:
- Pastel colors (looks romance, not thriller)
- Script fonts (looks cozy mystery, not intense)
- Central portraits (over-saturated market)

**Example prompt formula**:
```
Dark, atmospheric mystery thriller book cover, ominous [SYMBOL], 
deep navy and crimson color scheme, dramatic lighting, 
rain-soaked streets, strong shadows, professional typography space, 
high contrast, cinematic noir style, 8k quality
```

---

### **2. ROMANCE**
‚úÖ **Elements that convert**:
- **Vibrant, saturated colors** (jewel tones, pastels)
- **Human figures** (MUST show)
- **Serif or script titles** (Playfair Display, Dancing Script)
- **Soft lighting** (golden hour, dreamy bokeh)
- **Focal point**: Couple, single model + title synergy

‚ùå **Common failures**:
- No people (instant sales death)
- Generic stock photos (signals low-budget)
- Dark palettes (looks thriller, not romance)

---

### **3. SCI-FI**
‚úÖ **Elements that convert**:
- **Tech-forward typography** (Exo, Orbitron, Rajdhani)
- **Cosmic/metallic palettes** (electric blue, chrome, deep space)
- **Hard edges** (geometric shapes, clean lines)
- **Focal point**: Spaceship, planet, tech artifact
- **Lighting**: Neon, holographic, stark whites

‚ùå **Common failures**:
- Soft organic shapes (looks fantasy)
- Warm earth tones (looks historical)
- Serif fonts (looks literary)

---

### **4. FANTASY**
‚úÖ **Elements that convert**:
- **Ornate serif titles** (Cinzel, Trajan Pro)
- **Rich, saturated palettes** (deep purples, golds, forest greens)
- **Organic textures** (vines, stone, fabric)
- **Focal point**: Symbol, weapon, castle, mystical creature
- **Borders/frames** (signals epic scope)

‚ùå **Common failures**:
- Sans-serif fonts (looks sci-fi)
- Minimalism (signals literary, not epic)
- Modern tech elements (genre confusion)

---

### **5. LITERARY FICTION**
‚úÖ **Elements that convert**:
- **Simple, elegant typography** (Garamond, Baskerville)
- **Muted, sophisticated palettes** (sage, cream, charcoal)
- **Abstract or symbolic imagery** (NOT genre-specific)
- **Whitespace** (signals thoughtfulness)
- **Focal point**: Typography IS the art

‚ùå **Common failures**:
- Busy compositions (looks commercial)
- Bright colors (looks genre fiction)
- Literal imagery (too on-the-nose)

## üé® UNIVERSAL COVER DESIGN LAWS (NON-NEGOTIABLE)

### **Law #1: TITLE READABILITY TRUMPS ALL**
- **Test**: If unreadable at 200px wide ‚Üí FAIL
- **Solution**: 
  - High contrast (70%+ difference)
  - Clear font (no thin strokes)
  - No more than 3 words per line
  - Avoid texture overlap on title

### **Law #2: GENRE SIGNALS IN 0.3 SECONDS**
- **Test**: Remove title ‚Üí genre still obvious? ‚Üí PASS
- **Solution**:
  - Match genre color psychology
  - Use genre-appropriate typography
  - Include genre-specific symbols

### **Law #3: FOCAL HIERARCHY**
```
Priority 1: Title (60% visual weight)
Priority 2: Central image (30% visual weight)
Priority 3: Author name (10% visual weight)
```

### **Law #4: CONTRAST IS KING**
- **Minimum contrast ratio**: 4.5:1 (title vs background)
- **Ideal contrast ratio**: 7:1+
- **Test**: Grayscale version ‚Üí still impactful? ‚Üí PASS

### **Law #5: AVOID THE "COMMITTEE DESIGN" TRAP**
‚ùå Don't try to please everyone
‚úÖ Optimize for **one specific reader avatar**

## üö´ DEADLY MISTAKES (INSTANT SALES KILLERS)

### **1. Font Crimes**
‚ùå Comic Sans, Papyrus, Curlz
‚ùå More than 2 font families
‚ùå Low-contrast fonts (thin serif on textured background)
‚ùå Centered Trajan for non-fantasy

### **2. Color Crimes**
‚ùå All primary colors (looks children's book)
‚ùå Neon on neon (looks 1990s clip art)
‚ùå Brown + beige romance (looks historical)
‚ùå Pastel thriller (genre confusion)

### **3. Composition Crimes**
‚ùå Central portrait staring at camera (overdone)
‚ùå Clipart-style imagery (signals amateur)
‚ùå Busy background under title (readability death)
‚ùå No clear focal point (eye doesn't know where to land)

### **4. Typography Crimes**
‚ùå All lowercase title (looks indie poetry)
‚ùå Subtitle larger than title
‚ùå Text wrapping around images awkwardly
‚ùå Inconsistent kerning

## üîß TECHNICAL KDP REQUIREMENTS

### **Front Cover Dimensions** (eBook):
- **Minimum**: 1000px tall
- **Recommended**: 1600 x 2560px (2.5:4 ratio)
- **Maximum**: 10,000px either dimension
- **File size**: < 50MB
- **Format**: JPG or TIFF (JPG preferred)
- **Color**: RGB (NOT CMYK for eBook)

### **Print Cover Dimensions**:
- **Varies by trim size + page count**
- **Use KDP Cover Calculator**:
  https://kdp.amazon.com/en_US/cover-calculator
- **Bleed**: 0.125" (3.175mm) required
- **Safe zone**: 0.125" from trim edge
- **Barcode zone**: 2" x 1.2" bottom right (back only)
