# Privacy Policy - Artisan AI

**Effective Date: February 3, 2026**

## 1. Introduction

Artisan AI ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Service (the "Foundry").

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Information
- **Account Data:** Email address, name, and encrypted password hash.
- **Payment Data:** Transaction history (processed securely by Paddle; we do not store full credit card numbers).

### 2.2 Usage Data
- **Activity Logs:** Time spent, tools used, and credits consumed.
- **Generated Content:** Prompts, manuscripts, and images created within the Service.
- **Device Info:** IP address, browser type, and operating system (for security and anti-abuse).

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data strictly to:
- **Provide Services:** Authenticate access and deliver AI-generated content.
- **Process Payments:** Facilitate subscriptions and credit purchases.
- **Improve Reliability:** Monitor system performance and debug errors.
- **Enforce Security:** Detect and prevent fraudulent usage or "Ban Evasion".

## 4. AI & Data Processing

### 4.1 Third-Party AI Models
Our Service routes your inputs (prompts) to third-party AI providers, including:
- **Google Gemini (Vertex AI)** for text intelligence.
- **Fal.ai / Flux** for image generation.
- **HuggingFace** for utility models.

### 4.2 Data Privacy with AI
- **No Training:** We do NOT use your private manuscripts or proprietary inputs to train public AI models.
- **Ephemeral Processing:** AI providers typically process data ephemerally. Please review their respective privacy policies for details.

## 5. Data Retention

- **Active Accounts:** We retain your project data while your account is active.
- **Deleted Accounts:** Upon account deletion, we purge your personal data within 30 days. Some secure logs (e.g., payment records) may be retained for tax/legal compliance.

## 6. Security

We implement industrial-grade security measures:
- **Encryption:** Data in transit (SSL/TLS) and at rest (AES-256).
- **Access Control:** Strict role-based access for our internal staff.
- **Compliance:** We align with GDPR and CCPA standards.

## 7. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access:** Request a copy of your personal data.
- **Rectify:** Correct inaccurate information.
- **Delete:** Request total account deletion.
- **Export:** Download your projects in standard formats.

For privacy inquiries: **privacy@innerwinner.com**

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in our technology or legal requirements. We will notify you of significant changes via dashboard alerts.
